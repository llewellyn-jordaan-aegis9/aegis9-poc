Enterprise Security Architecture

Aegis9’s architectural team utilises a secure-by-design approach, therefore designing systems and environments to a pre-secured and compliant state, while achieving all business requirements. Being able to provide end to end security, from Intelligence through to Governance, Aegis9 personnel have a depth of understanding across all facets of security, and the ability to drive programs through to completion, and ensure they remain operationally secure after delivery. The designed security framework will provide for cyclical improvement so new security threats are identified, the impact assessed and addressed where required as they emerge.

Audit, Compliance and Programs

Aegis9 has extensive experience designing, implementing and managing comprehensive security programs to adhere to, or be certified to, a wide range of government and industry standards. These have been delivered to clients requiring additional assurance that their security programs meet certain standards or those providing services to government departments and agencies that require a range of standards be met as part of contractual obligations.

Aegis9 uses the P3M (Portfolio, Program and Project Management) methodologies to ensure when engaging with clients that required initiatives do not overly impact business-as-usual operations. This is achieved by ensuring that the Organisation’s change programs are approved and viable and directly align to business outcomes and business benefits.

Security Intelligence (Defend, Hunt, Attack)

Aegis9 provides security services at all stages during an environment’s lifespan. We will undertake a thorough assessment of the threat landscape and associated risk for your system. Controls are tailored, to ensure the security is as robust as necessary to protect your data. Aegis9 actively verifies your system’s security throughout the lifecycle, employing many operational controls such as penetration testing. Aegis9 responds to detected threats with their bespoke threat hunting team.

Aegis9 Security Intelligence has a comprehensive range of products, services and training that enable clients to develop and utilise OSINT to provide timely, relevant and accurate knowledge base in ways that identify threats and enhance security outcomes. These services are provided by Aegis9’s Security Intelligence staff who have extensive military, law enforcement and intelligence backgrounds. Their experience and skills support the delivery of these services to clients in government, military, law enforcement, intelligence, finance, insurance and recruitment agencies.
